## 2. Structure

This MSA governs the general terms under which Provider will supply services to Client. Specific engagements shall be described in one or more **Statements of Work** ("SOW") that reference this MSA. In case of conflict, the MSA prevails unless the SOW expressly overrides a term.

## 5. Term

This MSA shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for `[[TERM]]` (the "Initial Term"), and shall automatically renew for successive one-year periods unless either Party provides written notice of non-renewal at least ninety (90) days prior to the end of the then-current term.

## 6. Intellectual Property

- **Client Materials.** Client retains all right, title, and interest in materials it provides to Provider.
- **Deliverables.** Unless a SOW states otherwise, Provider assigns to Client all right, title, and interest in the deliverables created specifically for Client under a SOW, upon full payment.
- **Provider Background IP.** Provider retains ownership of its pre-existing tools, methodologies, and know-how. Provider grants Client a non-exclusive, perpetual, royalty-free license to use such background IP solely as incorporated in the deliverables.

## 7. Confidentiality

Each Party shall protect the other's Confidential Information with the same care it uses for its own, but no less than reasonable care. Confidentiality obligations survive for five (5) years after termination.

## 8. Warranties

Provider warrants that:

1. It has the corporate power and authority to enter this MSA;
2. Services will be performed in a professional and workmanship-like manner;
3. Deliverables will conform in material respects to the specifications in the applicable SOW; and
4. It will comply with all laws and regulations applicable to Provider's performance.

## 9. Indemnification

Provider shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Client from and against any third-party claims arising from Provider's (a) breach of this MSA, (b) negligence or willful misconduct, or (c) infringement of any third-party IP by the deliverables or the Services, subject to Client's reasonable cooperation.
