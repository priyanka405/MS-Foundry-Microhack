# Compliance Policy · Data, Sanctions, and Third-Party Risk

> **Policy ID:** `POL-COMPLIANCE-2026.07`
> **Owner:** Chief Compliance Office
> **Version:** 2026.07
> **Applies to:** All contracts and vendor engagements.

---

## 1. Purpose

Set the compliance guardrails every contract must satisfy: data protection, sanctions screening, third-party risk, and record-keeping.

## 2. Data classification

| Classification | Examples | Contract requirement |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Public** | Marketing collateral | Standard clauses |
| **Confidential** | Internal roadmaps, financials | NDA + confidentiality clause required |
| **Restricted** | Personal data, health, payment card, gov | DPA + data-processing clause + Compliance sign-off |

## 3. Personal data (PII)

- Any contract involving processing of personal data of EU / EEA / UK data subjects must include a **GDPR-compliant DPA**.
- Contracts involving personal data of California residents must reference **CCPA/CPRA** obligations.
- Contracts touching health data must include **HIPAA BAA** language where applicable.

## 4. Sensitive data protection

- Do not share raw restricted data with vendors unless a DPA is in place.
- Contracts must specify **data residency** (region), **retention period**, and **deletion obligations**.
- Provider must notify Client of any confirmed data incident within **72 hours** of discovery.

## 5. Sanctions and restricted parties

- Every counterparty must be screened against the current sanctions lists (OFAC / EU / UK / UN) prior to signature.
- Counterparties on the restricted list may not be contracted with, regardless of value.
- Screening results must be recorded in the vendor register.

## 6. Anti-bribery and anti-corruption (ABAC)

All contracts must include a clause requiring the counterparty to comply with applicable ABAC laws (US FCPA, UK Bribery Act, and equivalent local laws), and to permit audit rights on request.

## 7. Third-party risk assessment

Before onboarding, Procurement shall complete a **third-party risk assessment** covering:

- Cybersecurity posture (ISO 27001 / SOC 2 attestation).
- Financial stability (Dun &amp; Bradstreet or equivalent).
- Business continuity plan.
- Sub-processor list and change process.

## 8. Record-keeping

Contracts and associated approvals must be retained for **7 years** after termination, or longer if required by law.

## 9. Reporting

Suspected compliance breaches must be reported via the confidential Compliance Hotline within 24 hours of discovery.
