## Standard clause (verbatim — do NOT alter)

> **Payment Terms.** Client shall pay all undisputed invoices within **forty-five (45) calendar days** of Client's receipt of a valid, properly addressed invoice ("Net-45"). Invoices shall be delivered electronically to the address designated by Client. Fees are stated exclusive of applicable taxes; each Party is responsible for taxes imposed on its own net income. If Client disputes any portion of an invoice in good faith, Client shall pay the undisputed portion and provide written notice of the disputed portion within thirty (30) days of receipt of the invoice; the Parties shall then work in good faith to resolve the dispute within an additional thirty (30) days. Late undisputed amounts accrue interest at the lesser of one percent (1%) per month or the maximum rate permitted by law.

## Plain-English summary

- Standard payment term is **Net-45** from receipt of a valid invoice.
- Invoices go electronically to the address Client designates.
- Taxes: each side pays their own income tax.
- Disputes: pay the undisputed part; flag the disputed part in writing within 30 days.
- Interest on late (undisputed) payments: 1% / month or legal max.

## When to use

- Default choice for MSA, SOW, and vendor agreements.
- Do **not** substitute this with a hand-written variant.

## When to escalate

Requests for **shorter than Net-30** or **prepayment** must be escalated per the [Procurement Guidelines](../policies/procurement_guidelines.md).
