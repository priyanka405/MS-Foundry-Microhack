## 1. Parties

This Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement (the **"Agreement"**) is entered into as of **[Effective Date]** by and between:

- **[Company Legal Name]**, a company incorporated in **[Jurisdiction of Incorporation]**, with its registered office at **[Registered Address]** (**"Disclosing/Receiving Party A"**); and
- **[Counterparty Legal Name]**, a company incorporated in **[Jurisdiction of Incorporation]**, with its registered office at **[Registered Address]** (**"Disclosing/Receiving Party B"**).

Each a **"Party"**, and together the **"Parties"**.

## 2. Purpose

The Parties wish to explore a potential business relationship relating to **[Purpose — e.g., "the evaluation of X's supply-chain analytics platform for potential procurement by Y"]** (the **"Purpose"**), and in connection therewith may exchange Confidential Information.

## 3. Definition of Confidential Information

*"Confidential Information"* means any non-public information disclosed by one Party (the **"Discloser"**) to the other (the **"Recipient"**), in any form, that a reasonable person would understand to be confidential, including but not limited to: technical data, trade secrets, business plans, customer lists, pricing, product roadmaps, financial results, and source code.

## 4. Exclusions

Confidential Information does **not** include information that: (a) is or becomes publicly available without breach; (b) was rightfully known to Recipient before disclosure; (c) is rightfully obtained by Recipient from a third party without confidentiality obligations; or (d) is independently developed by Recipient without use of the Discloser's Confidential Information.

## 5. Obligations

The Recipient shall (i) protect Confidential Information with at least the same degree of care it uses for its own information of like importance, and in no event less than **reasonable care**; (ii) use it solely for the Purpose; and (iii) not disclose it except to employees, contractors, and professional advisors who need to know and are bound by confidentiality obligations no less protective than this Agreement.

## 6. Term

This Agreement enters into force on the Effective Date and continues for **[Term — default 2 years]**. Confidentiality obligations shall survive for **five (5) years** from the date of disclosure, or perpetually for trade secrets.

## 7. Return or Destruction

Upon written request or on termination, the Recipient shall promptly return or destroy all Confidential Information and certify such destruction in writing, subject to legal retention obligations and secure back-up archives.

## 8. Compelled Disclosure

If the Recipient is legally compelled to disclose Confidential Information, it shall (a) give prompt written notice to the Discloser, (b) cooperate in seeking a protective order, and (c) disclose only the minimum required.

## 9. No License

Nothing herein grants any license, title, or ownership in Confidential Information, other than the limited right to use it for the Purpose.

## 10. No Warranty

All Confidential Information is provided **"AS IS"**. Neither Party makes any warranty of accuracy or completeness.

## 11. Remedies

The Parties acknowledge that a breach of this Agreement may cause irreparable harm for which monetary damages would be inadequate; each Party shall be entitled to seek equitable relief in addition to any other remedies.

## 12. Governing Law and Jurisdiction

This Agreement is governed by the laws of **[Jurisdiction — default: Republic of Ireland]**. The Parties submit to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of **[Venue — default: Dublin]**.

## 13. General

This Agreement (a) constitutes the entire agreement on the subject matter, (b) may be amended only in writing signed by both Parties, (c) does not create a partnership, and (d) may be executed in counterparts, including by electronic signature.
