## 1. Parties and Effective Date

This Master Services Agreement (the **"Agreement"**) is entered into as of **[Effective Date]** by and between **[Company Legal Name]** (**"Customer"**) and **[Supplier Legal Name]** (**"Supplier"**).

## 2. Structure

This Agreement governs all Statements of Work (each a **"SOW"**) executed between the Parties. Each SOW incorporates this Agreement by reference. In the event of conflict, this Agreement prevails, except where the SOW expressly amends a specific provision.

## 3. Services and Deliverables

Supplier shall provide the services and deliverables described in each SOW (the **"Services"**), in accordance with the specifications, milestones, and acceptance criteria set out therein.

## 4. Fees and Payment

- Fees are set out in each SOW.
- Supplier invoices monthly in arrears.
- Customer pays undisputed invoices within **thirty (30) days** of receipt.
- Disputed amounts shall be identified in writing within fifteen (15) days; the undisputed portion is payable on the normal schedule.
- Late fees, if any, shall not exceed **the lower of 1% per month or the maximum permitted by law**.

> ⚠️ Do NOT modify payment terms below 30 days without Finance approval. See [`clause-library/payment.md`](../clause-library/payment.md).

## 5. Term and Termination

- Initial Term: **[Initial Term — default 3 years]** from the Effective Date.
- Renewal: automatic renewal for successive **one (1) year** terms unless either Party gives **ninety (90) days'** written notice of non-renewal.
- Termination for convenience: either Party may terminate on **sixty (60) days'** written notice.
- Termination for cause: either Party may terminate immediately for material breach that is uncured after **thirty (30) days'** written notice.

> ⚠️ Termination-for-convenience notice periods and cure periods are controlled clauses. See [`clause-library/termination.md`](../clause-library/termination.md).

## 6. Warranties

Supplier warrants that: (a) Services will be performed with professional skill and care; (b) Deliverables will materially conform to the specifications in the SOW; (c) Services will not infringe third-party IP; and (d) Supplier will comply with applicable laws.

## 7. Indemnification

See the approved indemnity clause in [`clause-library/indemnity.md`](../clause-library/indemnity.md). Any deviation requires Legal approval.

## 8. Limitation of Liability

See the approved liability clause in [`clause-library/liability.md`](../clause-library/liability.md). Any deviation requires Legal approval.

## 9. Confidentiality

Each Party's confidentiality obligations are those set out in the executed NDA between the Parties, or, if none, are hereby incorporated from the approved NDA template.

## 10. Data Protection

Supplier shall comply with applicable data-protection laws (including GDPR / UK GDPR / relevant local equivalents) and the Data Processing Addendum (**DPA**) attached to this Agreement if Supplier processes personal data on Customer's behalf.

## 11. Intellectual Property

- **Pre-existing IP** remains with the Party that owns it.
- **Deliverables** are assigned to Customer on payment in full, subject to Supplier's underlying pre-existing IP and generally applicable tools/methodologies.
- Supplier grants Customer a perpetual, worldwide, royalty-free license to use Supplier's pre-existing IP embedded in Deliverables for Customer's internal business purposes.

## 12. Subcontracting

Supplier may not subcontract material portions of the Services without Customer's prior written consent, which shall not be unreasonably withheld. Supplier remains fully responsible for its subcontractors.

## 13. Insurance

Supplier shall maintain, at minimum: (a) general liability insurance of **€2,000,000** per occurrence; (b) professional indemnity insurance of **€5,000,000** per claim; (c) cyber liability insurance of **€2,000,000** aggregate, where Supplier processes Customer data.

## 14. Governing Law and Dispute Resolution

This Agreement is governed by the laws of **[Jurisdiction — default: Republic of Ireland]**. Disputes shall first be escalated between senior executives; failing resolution within thirty (30) days, disputes shall be finally settled by the courts of **[Venue — default: Dublin]**.

## 15. General

Notices, assignment, entire agreement, severability, force majeure, and counterparts clauses are the standard Legal Ops boilerplate — do not modify without Legal review.
