# Approved Clause — Payment Terms

> **Clause ID:** `CLL-PAY-v1.3`
> **Applies to:** MSA, SOW
> **Owner:** Finance + Legal Ops
> **Last reviewed:** 2025-03-15

## Approved wording (verbatim)

> **Fees.** Fees for the Services shall be as set out in each SOW. Unless otherwise agreed in the SOW, fees are quoted in **EUR**, exclusive of VAT and any other applicable taxes, which shall be added at the rate in force at the time of invoice.
>
> **Invoicing.** Supplier shall invoice Customer monthly in arrears, in electronic form, referencing the SOW number and the milestones or period covered. Invoices for milestone-based work shall be issued only upon acceptance of the corresponding Deliverable in accordance with the SOW.
>
> **Payment.** Customer shall pay each undisputed invoice within **thirty (30) days** of receipt to the bank account nominated by Supplier in writing. Payment shall be made in the invoiced currency by electronic transfer.
>
> **Disputes.** If Customer disputes any portion of an invoice, Customer shall notify Supplier in writing within **fifteen (15) days** of receipt, specifying the disputed amount and the basis for the dispute. The undisputed portion shall be paid on the normal schedule. The Parties shall use good-faith efforts to resolve disputed amounts within thirty (30) days of notification.
>
> **Late Payment.** Undisputed amounts not paid within thirty (30) days of the due date shall accrue interest at **the lower of one percent (1%) per month or the maximum rate permitted by applicable law**, calculated from the due date until paid in full. Supplier shall not suspend Services for late payment without first providing **fifteen (15) days'** written notice and an opportunity for Customer to cure.
>
> **Expenses.** Reasonable, pre-approved travel and out-of-pocket expenses shall be reimbursable at cost, without markup, and subject to Customer's expense policy provided in advance.

## Rationale

- **Net 30** aligns with Finance's working-capital assumptions in the annual plan.
- **15-day dispute window** matches the AR team's SLA and avoids stale disputes.
- **1% / month, capped by law** — commercially reasonable and enforceable across EU and UK.
- **No suspension without notice** — protects continuity of business-critical services.

## Deviations that MUST be flagged

The CLM Agent flags any of the following:

- Payment window shorter than **15 days** or longer than **90 days**.
- **Prepayment** in excess of 20% of the SOW value without CFO Office approval.
- Late-payment interest above **1.5% per month**.
- **Suspension of Services** on late payment without a cure window.
- Markup on expenses (line item: "administrative fee", "handling", etc.).
- Currency of payment inconsistent with SOW currency.

## When a deviation is acceptable

- **Net 45** for large enterprises with matching commitment volumes (needs Finance sign-off).
- **Prepayment** on multi-year subscription-style commitments where Supplier grants a material discount.
- Alternative currencies for local subsidiaries, provided FX risk is documented.
